# Epic: Doctor Appointment Management System

## Epic ID
EPIC-001

## Epic Name
Doctor Appointment Management System

## Description
Build a comprehensive doctor appointment management system that enables patients to book appointments with doctors, manage their medical records, and facilitate communication between healthcare providers and patients.

## Business Value
- Streamline appointment scheduling process
- Reduce administrative overhead for healthcare facilities
- Improve patient experience and accessibility to healthcare services
- Minimize appointment no-shows through automated reminders
- Enable better time management for healthcare providers

## Success Criteria
- Patients can successfully book, reschedule, and cancel appointments
- Doctors can manage their availability and view scheduled appointments
- System sends automated notifications and reminders
- User satisfaction rate > 85%
- Reduction in appointment scheduling time by 50%

## Target Users
- Patients seeking medical appointments
- Doctors and healthcare providers
- Healthcare facility administrators
- Medical staff and receptionists

## Key Features
- Patient registration and profile management
- Doctor profile and specialty listing
- Appointment booking and scheduling
- Calendar integration
- Notification system (email, SMS)
- Medical records management
- Payment processing
- Admin dashboard for facility management

## Dependencies
- Healthcare facility infrastructure
- Payment gateway integration
- SMS/Email service providers
- Compliance with healthcare regulations (HIPAA, GDPR)
